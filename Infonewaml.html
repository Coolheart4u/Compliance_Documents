Νέα Διευκόλυνση Επικαιροποίησης Στοιχείων Πελατών
New Customer Information Update Facilitation

Ισχύει από: 2 Ιουνίου 2025 / Effective from: June 2, 2025

1. Επικαιροποίηση βάσει κινδύνου / Risk-Based Updates

What is Expected: Updates will depend on customer risk level. No fixed interval for low-risk customers.

Example: A customer with stable income, known profile, and no suspicious transactions.

Challenge: Risk of outdated/incomplete customer records due to infrequent review.

Solution:

  • Set system reminders every 18–24 months.
  • Update risk files with each new interaction.
  • Use event-based triggers (e.g., new employer) for review.
Required for Implementation: IT Support, Legal Support, Central Bank of Cyprus.

2. Δεκτή η χρήση αντιγράφων ταυτότητας / Use of Identity Copies Accepted

What is Expected: Clear ID copies (e.g., via email or app) are acceptable.

Example: Customer sends a scanned photo of their ID.

Challenge: Increased risk of fake or altered documents.

Solution:

  • Compare with existing file records.
  • Request live video confirmation if unsure.
  • Utilize fraud detection tools.
Required for Implementation: IT Support, Legal Support, Central Bank of Cyprus.

3. Χρήση υφιστάμενων πληροφοριών / Use of Existing Information

What is Expected: Recently submitted valid documents do not need to be resubmitted.

Example: A payslip from two months ago used in a loan application.

Challenge: Risk of using outdated or unrelated documents.

Solution:

  • Set document validity periods (e.g., 3 months for payslips).
  • Create an “Active KYC Documents” folder in CRM.
  • Ensure relevance to reassessment purpose.
Required for Implementation: IT Support, Legal Support, Central Bank of Cyprus.

4. Ευελιξία για πελάτες με προβλήματα υγείας / Flexibility for Customers with Health Issues

What is Expected: Authorized third parties may submit documents on behalf of the customer.

Example: Elderly client authorizes a family member to deliver documents.

Challenge: Submissions without valid authorization or identity confirmation.

Solution:

  • Request signed authorization or power of attorney.
  • Confirm identity through gov.cy or official apps.
Required for Implementation: IT Support, Legal Support, Central Bank of Cyprus.

5. Ευελιξία στην απόδειξη διεύθυνσης / Flexibility in Proof of Address

What is Expected: Accept utility bills or official documents from electronic sources.

Example: AHK electricity bill PDF or gov.cy certificate.

Challenge: Risk of falsified documents.

Solution:

  • Prioritize documents from official websites.
  • Check metadata or verify via official email.
Required for Implementation: IT Support, Legal Support, Central Bank of Cyprus.

6. Πιστοποίηση από τρίτους ή μέσω ηλεκτρονικών μέσων / Certification by Third Parties or Electronically

What is Expected: Acceptance of certified documents from public authorities or digital sources.

Example: Identity certificate from KEP or document from gov.cy.

Challenge: Difficulty confirming source authenticity.

Solution:

  • Maintain a list of approved certifying bodies.
  • Confirm validity through calls or official emails.
Required for Implementation: IT Support, Legal Support, Central Bank of Cyprus.

Τελική Υπενθύμιση / Final Reminder

These new facilitation measures do not override regulatory obligations. They require proper documentation and a correct implementation of the risk-based approach.
