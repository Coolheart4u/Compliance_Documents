# Directive Area Existing Directive Updated Directive Gap Identified Impact Recommended Action Priority
1 Customer Due Diligence (CDD) Basic CDD for low-risk customers with no expiry on verification documents. Mandatory periodic review of all CDD files every 3 years regardless of risk level. No automated review cycle in current system. Regulatory breach; missed reviews. Update AML system to trigger reviews; revise CDD policy. High
2 Enhanced Due Diligence (EDD) EDD required only for PEPs and high-risk jurisdictions. Expanded to include crypto-related businesses and NGOs operating internationally. EDD checklist does not include these new categories. Incomplete risk coverage. Update customer risk assessment model and EDD workflows. High
3 Beneficial Ownership (BO) Identification of BO above 25% threshold. Threshold reduced to 10% for high-risk customers. Current onboarding system set to 25% BO trigger. System non-compliance; onboarding risk. Modify onboarding forms and AML engine logic. Critical
4 Ongoing Monitoring Manual transaction review for high-risk accounts monthly. Real-time automated monitoring mandated for all high-risk clients. Lack of real-time monitoring tool. Risk of undetected suspicious activity. Procure or upgrade transaction monitoring system. Critical
5 Suspicious Transaction Reporting (STR) Reports submitted to MOKAS within 7 days of internal escalation. Deadline shortened to 3 business days. Internal SLA not aligned with regulatory timeline. Late STR submission â€“ legal risk. Revise SOPs and alert escalation process. High
6 Training Annual AML training mandatory. Training frequency increased to bi-annual with testing and certification. No training calendar or test mechanisms in place. Non-compliance; audit issues. Create structured training program with LMS tracking. Medium
7 Record Keeping Retention for 5 years after account closure. Extended to 7 years for high-risk accounts. Retention schedule in DMS not updated. Potential data loss or deletion risk. Update document management policy and archiving setup. Medium
8 Risk-Based Approach Applied at onboarding only. Must be dynamic and reviewed annually. Static risk rating without lifecycle updates. Weak customer risk profiling. Implement periodic risk review mechanism. High
