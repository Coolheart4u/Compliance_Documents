Mitigating Measures

To manage and reduce risk, organizations can implement the following measures:

üîπ Transaction Monitoring: Establish automated systems to flag irregular transactions such as large, unexplained fund movements or high-frequency payments.

üîπ Sanctions Screening: Continuously screen customers and counterparties against international sanctions lists (OFAC, EU, UN, UK, FATF).

üîπ Ultimate Beneficial Ownership (UBO) Transparency: Require full disclosure of ownership structures, including nominee shareholders and offshore entities.

üîπ Regular Risk Reassessment: Implement periodic reviews of customers' risk profiles to adapt to changes in their business activities or regulatory status.

üîπ Legal & Compliance Oversight: Require senior management and legal teams to review high-risk cases before approval.

üîπ Whistleblower & Reporting Mechanisms: Encourage anonymous reporting of suspicious activities within the organization.
