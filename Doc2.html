Entity & Individual Risk Assessment & Scoring Matrix

Evaluating risk for onboarding entities and individuals with potential high-risk factors.

Risk Categories & Scoring

Risk Factor Low Risk (1) Medium Risk (2) High Risk (3)
Jurisdiction EU, UK, US, AU, SG, Regulated OECD countries Partially regulated (UAE, HK, CA, LATAM) Sanctioned or High-Risk Non-EU (Iran, North Korea, Russia, Syria)
Sanctions Exposure No listings or indirect exposure Listed in non-EU but not primary jurisdiction Listed by major authorities (UN, US OFAC, UK, EU, FATF Blacklist)
Connections to PEPs No PEPs involved Distant or indirect association Direct PEP involvement, high-risk political ties

Scoring & Decision Guidelines

  • Low Risk (7-10 points): ✅ Approved with standard monitoring.
    • No additional verification beyond standard AML/KYC procedures.
    • Regular transaction monitoring for deviations.
  • Medium Risk (11-16 points): ⚠️ Enhanced Due Diligence (EDD) required.
    • Perform additional background checks.
    • Obtain documentation for source of funds and ownership.
  • High Risk (17+ points): ❌ Reject or impose strict compliance conditions.
    • Legal clearance and senior management approval required.
    • Real-time transaction monitoring.
